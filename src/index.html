About Us

SEC Owners Suzie and Jeff Hansen

Welcome to Sunset Equestrian Center (SEC), a family-friendly, full-service facility nestled in a beautiful suburban equestrian community in West Kaysville, Utah. We offer indoor and outdoor stalls with automatic waterers, spacious feeders, and stall mats for your convenience, along with nine stalls featuring attached paddocks for extra comfort. Our barn stays cool in the summer and is heated in the winter, ensuring year-round comfort. You'll find hot and cold water available in our wash stall area at all times. Outside, we offer covered stalls with paddocks as well as large corrals with shelters.

At SEC, boarders enjoy a spacious indoor arena with top-notch footing, two expansive outdoor arenas, seven turnout areas, two walkers, and two round pens for various riding activities. Scenic riding trails are easily accessible from our property. Adjacent to the indoor arena, our large office and viewing area is perfect for hosting events and birthday parties, and a playground is available for young children. Our experienced trainers provide riding lessons and horse training for all skill levels. We invite you to visit and experience everything we offer!
